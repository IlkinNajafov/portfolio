What was my responsibilities?

Istanbul... One of the beautiful city in the world. I worked independently and on a team to solve students' language problems and tried my best to serve as a friendly, hardworking, and punctual volunteer. We were teaching and helping to adults, students and specially to cute kids with their language barrier. Our purpose was to make them think in English and make them happy. We acquired it by playing games and some summer activities with them. I served the months of June, July, August. I wish, I could stay more but I couldn't. I needed to come back to my country to continue my education. It was amazing experience in my life.
